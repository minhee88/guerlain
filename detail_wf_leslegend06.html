Les Légendaires

Perplexing women, captivating men, Jicky speaks to the contradiction in all of us. A startling lemon-lavender fougère accord echoes the deeply personal - and unrequited - love story that inspired Perfumer Aimé Guerlain - before woody, creamy, vanilla notes bring warmth, character and resolution.

    1. Product Details

      THE COLLECTION’S ICONIC FRAGRANCE SELECTION
      A selection of L’Art & La Matière’s iconic scents offering an array of perfume creations to discover and wear as you wish. A fragrance journey among the most beautiful masterpieces crafted by Guerlain Perfumers, featuring two fresh scents - Musc Outreblanc, Herbes Troublantes, three ambery scents - Spiritueuse Double Vanille, Cuir Béluga, Angélique Noire, two floral scents - Rose Barbare, Néroli Outrenoir and one woody scent – Oud Nude.

      L’Art & La Matière is a collection of olfactory masterpieces expertly crafted by the House of Guerlain Perfumers. As artists guided by their creative genius, they shape the most beautiful materials to create daring works with talent and passion.
