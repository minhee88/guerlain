ART & GIFTING

For Guerlain, gifting is an art and art is an emotion. The Maison elevates the gesture of gift-giving to an elegant art form infused with unique savoir-faire and a genuine commitment to nature. Guerlain has conceived two eco-designed gift boxes, both sharing the same high quality standards and attention to detail. Whether celebrating a special occasion or simply for the pleasure of giving, the luxury of choice is integral to the art of gift-giving at Guerlain.

THE ICONIC BOX Each element of ‘The Iconic Gift Box’ has been carefully thought through to make your gift unique with a commitment to sustainable luxury. Much like skilled artisans, our experts lovingly craft your gift. From the tissue paper to the ribbon, they adorn it with the most precious of details.

DESIRABILITY AND COMMITMENT Crafted from FSC-certified¹ cardboard and paper sourced from well managed forests – using minimal glue and no magnets – this recyclable² case has been designed to reduce its impact while remaining refined. The tissue paper is 100% made in France, the glue is water-based and solvent-free. This timelessly elegant case can be reused as a sophisticated storage box. ¹FSC® defines standards based on agreed principles for responsible forest stewardship that are supported by environmental, social, and economic stakeholders. To learn more, visit www.fsc.org. ²See local recycling guidelines

THE MINIMAL BOX With the same high quality and desirability standards that mark each of its creations, Guerlain offers you an innovative alternative for even more responsible shipping. Made from a single material and fully recyclable, it contains at least 91% recycled materials and has been designed down to the smallest detail to reduce the amount of material.
